About Us

Healing Lives, Building Doctors

Viraat Ramayan Institute of Medical Sciences (VRIMS) is a premier NMC-recognised medical institution located at Koyala Belwa, Chakia, East Champaran, Bihar. We blend cutting-edge clinical care with rigorous academic training.

Our sprawling campus houses a 500-bed teaching hospital, state-of-the-art laboratories, a central library, hostels, and all facilities required for holistic medical education.

ICU Services

General Surgery

Laparoscopic & open surgeries by experienced surgical teams.

Joint replacement, fracture management, and spine care services.

Child healthcare from newborn to adolescence including vaccination.

Maternal care, high-risk pregnancy, and minimal-access gynecology.

Ophthalmology

LASIK, cataract surgery, and complete eye care with modern equipment.

Radiology

Blood Bank

Pharmacy

In-house pharmacy with all essential medicines at affordable prices.

Dialysis Unit

Dental Services
