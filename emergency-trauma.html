What We Offer

Emergency Services

Trauma Resuscitation

Advanced trauma life support (ATLS) protocols with dedicated trauma bays and rapid-response teams.

Cardiac Emergency

24×7 ECG, thrombolysis, and cardiac monitoring. Cardiologist on call for chest pain & MI management.

Neuro Emergency

Stroke protocol, CT-guided triage, neurosurgeon on-call for head injuries and CNS emergencies.

Pediatric Emergency

Dedicated pediatric emergency bay with trained neonatologist and pediatric intensivist support.

Obstetric Emergency

High-risk obstetric emergencies managed with direct OT access and NICU backup.

Burns Management

Dedicated burns management unit with dressing room, fluid resuscitation protocol, and ICU referral.

Ortho Trauma

Poly-trauma management, fracture splinting, traction, and emergency orthopaedic surgeries.

Toxicology / Poisoning

Overdose & poisoning management with antidote protocol, gastric lavage, and ICU escalation.

Respiratory Emergency

Nebulisation, mechanical ventilation, NIV therapy for asthma, COPD, and acute respiratory failure.

Infrastructure

Facilities & Equipment

Fully Equipped Trauma Bay

4 dedicated trauma resuscitation bays with crash cart, defibrillator, and airway management tools.

Digital X-Ray & CT (24×7)

On-site digital radiography and CT scanner for rapid diagnostic imaging round the clock.

Emergency OT

Dedicated emergency operation theatre adjacent to the casualty for immediate surgical interventions.

Point-of-Care Lab

Rapid ABG, troponin, lactate, and blood glucose testing available inside the emergency department.

Ventilator Support

Bedside mechanical ventilators and BiPAP/CPAP machines available in emergency observation area.

Blood Bank Interface

Direct interface with 24×7 blood bank for rapid cross-matching and emergency transfusion.

Triage System

Colour-coded triage (Red / Yellow / Green) managed by trained triage nurses at the entry point.

Ambulance Fleet

8 fully-equipped ambulances with ALS capabilities, oxygen, and trained paramedics on board.

Clinical Protocols

Emergency Protocols in Place

Code Blue

Cardiac / Respiratory Arrest — full resuscitation team activation within 2 minutes anywhere in the hospital.

Trauma Code

Polytrauma activation — simultaneous mobilisation of surgery, ortho, anaesthesia & radiology.

Stroke Protocol

Door-to-CT within 25 minutes. Thrombolysis decision within 60 minutes of arrival.

Neonatal Resuscitation

NALS-trained team available for all emergency deliveries and neonatal distress situations.

Mass Casualty Incident

MCI plan with surge capacity, field triage, and coordination with district health authorities.

Isolation Protocol

Dedicated isolation room for suspected infectious cases with PPE and negative-pressure management.
