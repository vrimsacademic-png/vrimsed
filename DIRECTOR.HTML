    Academic Excellence & Clinical Skill — The Twin Pillars

    Academic excellence and clinical skill are not separate pursuits — they are the twin pillars upon which every great physician is built. At VRIMS, we are committed to nurturing both with equal rigour and passion.

    — Director, Viraat Ramayan Institute of Medical Sciences

    Dear Students, Patients, Faculty, and Well-Wishers,

    It is with immense pride and a deep sense of responsibility that I address you as the Director of Viraat Ramayan Institute of Medical Sciences (VRIMS). Since our inception, we have held firm to the belief that quality healthcare and quality medical education are inseparable — each strengthens the other, and together they define who we are as an institution.

    VRIMS stands at a unique confluence of heritage and modernity. Nestled in the heart of East Champaran — a region steeped in history and rich in human spirit — our institution serves a community that has long awaited access to world-class healthcare. Every bed in our 500-bed teaching hospital, every lecture in our modern classrooms, and every experiment in our state-of-the-art laboratories is a step toward fulfilling that promise.

    Our goal is simple yet profound: to produce doctors who are not merely technically proficient, but deeply compassionate — physicians who understand that behind every patient is a family, a hope, and a story deserving of dignity and the very best of care.

    The NMC recognition we have earned is not just a certificate on the wall — it is a testament to the unwavering commitment of our faculty, staff, and management to meet and surpass the highest standards of medical education. We have invested in infrastructure, recruited experienced and dedicated faculty, and built robust clinical training programmes that expose our students to the full breadth of medical practice from day one.

    As we move forward, our vision encompasses growth across all dimensions — expanding our postgraduate programmes, strengthening our research output, forging national and international collaborations, and continually upgrading our clinical facilities. We are also deeply invested in community outreach, conducting health camps, awareness programmes, and telemedicine services that bring healthcare to the doorsteps of those who need it most.

    To our students: you have chosen one of the noblest professions in the world. The journey is demanding, but it is also profoundly rewarding. Use every opportunity VRIMS offers you — every clinical posting, every lecture, every interaction with a patient — as a building block toward becoming the doctor you aspire to be. We, the faculty and administration, are here to support, guide, and challenge you every step of the way.

    To our patients and their families: you place your trust in us at your most vulnerable moments, and we honour that trust with every ounce of our ability and care. Your health and well-being remain our highest priority.

    I invite all stakeholders — students, faculty, patients, and the community — to join us in building an institution that Bihar can be proud of for generations to come.
